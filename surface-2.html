// FAQ

Likely questions, plainly answered.

01

What's a "qualifying trade"?

Any trade ≥ $25 USDC, held for at least 24 hours. Below that, it accrues 0 points but still attributes you to @basedchad — your future trades still count.

02

Do I need a wallet?

No. Privy provisions one from your email. Power users connect their own. Polymarket needs a wallet on the other side either way — the wallet is invisible to you if you don't want it.

03

Is this a token sale?

No. Points are non-transferable. They convert pro-rata to $BLINQ at TGE. No money in to play. Trading on Polymarket is real money, separately.

04

What about sybil farms?

Discretionary sweep before TGE snapshot. Identity-bound accounts (Twitter + Gitcoin Passport) earn a 1.2× multiplier and are screened first. Flagged accounts are hidden from the public board.

05

I'm geo-blocked. Now what?

Polymarket isn't available in the US and a handful of other regions. You'll see an alternative-routes page if your IP is flagged. Attribution still locks for future use.

No hype. Just points, trades, and a real allocation.
